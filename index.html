About Me

Hi, I’m Nileena, a third-year Computer Science Engineering student at Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science. I love building things that work — and sometimes break them just to understand why.

I work with Python, C, Java, and React, and I enjoy the challenge of turning abstract ideas into functional, user-friendly software. Whether I’m debugging for hours or finally watching the code run perfectly, I’m always learning something new — and loving it.

When I’m not immersed in code, I’m probably imagining what the world would look like if apps could read minds (don’t worry, I’m not building one — yet).

Let’s connect — who knows, we might just build the next big thing.
